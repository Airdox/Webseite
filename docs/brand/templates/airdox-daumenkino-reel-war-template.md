## Creative Transformation Rule

Do not rebuild Vorlage 1 as-is.

Keep:

- white AIRDOX graffiti mass
- subtle cyan edge energy
- black technical background
- strong first-frame readability

Change:

- replace clean static logo placement with impact-driven beat cuts
- add pink/lime conflict accents as short attacks, not full-surface recolors
- add torn scanlines, slice-shifts, sticker-impact shadows, and audio-spine motion
- make the CTA `www.airdox.info` visible as an intentional campaign message
- keep all public-facing text clean; no internal draft, approval, or agent labels

## Format

- Canvas: 1080x1920
- Ratio: 9:16
- Duration targets: 5s draft, 15s hook, 30s post, 59s set promo
- Video: H.264 MP4, 30fps
- Audio: AAC, 48 kHz
- Preview: PNG around second 2 or 4, depending on the drop

## Layout

- Top zone: small AIRDOX identity marker, not a large headline.
- Main zone: oversized AIRDOX graffiti lockup with beat-hit distortion.
- Hook zone: one short command line, max 2 lines.
- Audio zone: 12-24 bar audio spine or waveform marker.
- CTA zone: `FULL SET: www.airdox.info`, above TikTok lower UI.
- Footer zone: set id, date, or track moment in muted text.

Safe-area minimum:

- Keep core text inside x=72..1008.
- Keep CTA above y=1630.
- Do not place required text in the right-side TikTok action rail.

Black technical field, short scanline tear, low-opacity Daumenkino fragments.

0.6s-1.5s:
AIRDOX fragments slam together from left/right slices. Do not reveal the full mark cleanly until the first beat hit.

1.5s-2.8s:
`REEL WAR` and `NO WARM-UP` hit in two separate frames. Add one pink or lime impact frame only on peaks.

2.8s-4.2s:
Logo locks clean enough to read. Audio spine becomes visible and drives scale/glow.

4.2s-end:
CTA locks: `FULL SET: www.airdox.info`. Keep it stable for at least 1.2s.
